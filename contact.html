Frequently asked questions

Transparent pricing and easier access for people who need help with a legal issue but do not want to start with a full law firm engagement.
How much does Dicta cost?
The assistant is free to use. Templates typically range from $49–$79, and bundles provide more coverage at a lower combined cost. If you need legal advice, consultations with a licensed attorney are available for a flat $99.
Why not just start with a law firm?
Many everyday legal questions do not require a full law firm engagement. Dicta helps you start with resources and attorney-drafted templates before deciding whether legal advice is necessary.
Who is Dicta built for?
Dicta is built for individuals, families, tenants, consumers, caregivers, and anyone facing a practical legal problem who wants faster access to information and next steps.
Does the AI provide legal advice?
No. The assistant surfaces public guidance, summarizes materials, and helps organize your issue. Legal advice is provided only by licensed attorneys after formal engagement.
