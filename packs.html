Frequently asked questions

Transparent pricing and practical tools for teams that need niche digital health and privacy support without starting with a full law firm engagement.
How much does Pixelwise cost?
The assistant is free to use for issue intake and resource discovery. Templates start lower, while templates paired with playbooks cost more because they include more structured guidance and usually provide better overall value than assembling materials one by one.
What is the difference between a template and a playbook?
A template gives you ready-to-use language. A playbook helps you decide which language to use, when to use it, and how different approaches line up with different business goals and risk tolerance levels.
Why are the templates + playbooks packages featured first?
Because most buyers do not just need a document — they need help using it well. Pairing templates with playbooks gives more guidance, reduces guesswork, and generally delivers better value than piecing materials together separately.
Does the AI provide legal advice?
No. The assistant helps organize issues, surface public guidance, and point you toward relevant templates or playbooks. It does not provide legal advice or create an attorney-client relationship.
