Frequently asked questions

Pixelwise is designed for founders, operators, and in‑house generalists who need fast answers on privacy, AI, and digital compliance without the friction of a traditional law firm engagement.
Why not just hire a law firm?
Many privacy and digital compliance questions don’t require a full law firm engagement. Pixelwise helps you start with resources, templates, and structured issue intake before deciding whether legal advice is necessary.
How much does this cost?
Most users begin with free AI‑assisted issue intake and resource discovery. Templates typically range from $59–$129, and bundles combine multiple documents at a discounted rate. If you need legal advice, consultations with a licensed attorney are available for a flat $199.
Who is Pixelwise designed for?
Pixelwise is especially useful for founders, product teams, marketers, and in‑house legal or compliance generalists who occasionally need niche expertise in areas like HIPAA, AI governance, marketing compliance, and digital tracking technologies.
Does the AI provide legal advice?
No. The assistant surfaces public regulatory guidance, summarizes materials, and helps organize your issue. Legal advice is provided only by licensed attorneys after formal engagement.
When should I speak with an attorney?
If your issue involves regulatory risk, contractual obligations, or a product launch decision, a short consultation can provide clarity quickly. The assistant helps organize the issue first so attorney time is focused and efficient.
