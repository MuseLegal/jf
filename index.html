Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to decide whether Pixelwise is the right fit for your team right now.
Who is Pixelwise for?
Pixelwise is for digital health, wellness, product, marketing, privacy, and in-house legal teams that keep running into recurring privacy questions and want stronger materials than generic templates without sending every issue to outside counsel.
Should I buy a focused topic or everything combined?
Buy a focused topic if your current pain point is mainly one area, like tracking tech or healthcare communications. Choose everything combined if your team expects recurring questions across multiple privacy workstreams.
What kinds of issues is Pixelwise best for?
Pixelwise is best for recurring privacy and digital health issues like website privacy policies, tracking-tech disclosures, HIPAA-facing vendor terms, AI governance materials, consent and marketing questions, and other repeat issues where teams need a practical answer and reusable materials.
When is Pixelwise not enough on its own?
Pixelwise is not a substitute for bespoke legal advice on your most sensitive matters. If you are dealing with litigation, a government investigation, a major transaction, a novel high-stakes issue, or facts that require deep legal judgment specific to your business, a law firm may still be the right next step.
Does Pixelwise create an attorney-client relationship?
No. Pixelwise provides general informational resources, templates, and playbooks. It does not create an attorney-client relationship, provide legal advice, or establish privilege.
